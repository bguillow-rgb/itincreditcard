Privacy Policy

Last updated June 15, 2026

ITINCreditCard.com ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices. By using this site you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

  • Information you give us. When you submit our card-matching or contact forms, we collect details such as your name, email, phone number, state, the type of credit card you're seeking, and any optional details you provide (such as a credit-score range and whether you have an ITIN only or an ITIN and an SSN).
  • Automatic information. Like most websites, we collect standard log and analytics data, IP address, browser type, pages viewed, and referring site, through tools such as Google Analytics.
  • Cookies. We and our partners use cookies and similar technologies for analytics and advertising. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

How we use your information

  • To match you with third-party card issuers and partners you ask to be connected with.
  • To respond to your questions and provide customer support.
  • To operate, measure, and improve the site.
  • To serve relevant advertising and comply with the law.

Sharing your information

When you submit a card-matching request, we share the details you provide with one or more card issuers or lead partners so they can contact you about credit card options. We may receive a referral fee or other compensation when we do this. Under some state privacy laws (such as California's CCPA/CPRA), this kind of sharing for compensation may be considered a "sale" or "sharing" of personal information. You can opt out at any time by emailing us, and we will not share your details with partners going forward. We may also share data with service providers (e.g., analytics, hosting, email) who act on our behalf, and we may disclose information if required by law.

Advertising & Google AdSense

We use third-party advertising, including Google AdSense. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visits. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Your choices & rights

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you, or opt out of future contact, by contacting us. Depending on where you live (for example, California or the EU), you may have additional rights under laws such as the CCPA or GDPR.

Data security & children

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your information but no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure. This site is not directed to children under 18, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes & contact

We may update this policy from time to time; the "last updated" date above reflects the latest version. Questions? Email us.
